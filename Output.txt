Toyota Tundra is a pickup truck manufactured in the United States by the Japanese manufacturer Toyota since May 1999. 
Tundra was the first North American full-size pickup to be built by a Japanese manufacturer.
Tundra was nominated for the North American Truck of the Year award 
and was Motor Trend magazine's Truck of the Year in 2000 and 2008. 
Initially built in a new Toyota plant in Princeton, Indiana, production was consolidated 
in 2008 to Toyota's San Antonio, Texas, factory and is the only full-size pickup truck manufactured in Texas.
